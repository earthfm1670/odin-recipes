Pizza Recipe

The Perfect Pizza

The Pizza that everyone is dying for

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cheese
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Prepare your flour
  2. Toss your flour into a circle pizza-like shape
  3. Fold the edge of your pizza to form a crust
  4. Prepare your topping of choice
  5. Spread your toppings on the pizza
  6. Cook the pizza in the oven for 15 minutes
